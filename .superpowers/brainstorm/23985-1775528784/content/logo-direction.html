Logo Direction for MemoryAisle

What visual personality should the logo convey? Pick the direction that feels most like the brand.

A

Mira-First

The logo IS Mira. The 5-bar waveform + sparkle becomes the brand mark. "MemoryAisle" in clean type next to it. The app icon is just the waveform on indigo-black. Bold, distinctive, instantly recognizable.

B

Abstract Wellness

A minimal abstract mark -- think a stylized "M" or a body silhouette made of flowing lines. Violet gradient. Modern health/fitness vibe. Professional but could blend in with other wellness apps.

C

Typography-Led

No icon. "MemoryAisle" in a custom serif/sans combination. The "M" or "A" has a subtle design detail (violet accent, waveform integrated into a letter). Elegant, editorial, like a luxury wellness brand.

D

Hybrid: Mira + Type

Mira's sparkle integrated into the wordmark itself. The dot on the "i" in "Aisle" becomes the four-point star. The waveform bars sit subtly under or beside the text. Feels premium and cohesive without being "just an icon."
