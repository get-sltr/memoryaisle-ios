# 2. PRIVACY POLICY

**Effective Date:** April 6, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

## 2.1 Overview

SLTR Digital LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the MemoryAisle mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## 2.2 Information We Collect

### 2.2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, name (optional), and authentication credentials via Amazon Cognito
- **Profile Information:** Dietary preferences, allergies, dietary restrictions, training schedule, health goals
- **Medication Information:** GLP-1 medication type, dosage, administration method (injection or oral), dosing schedule. THIS INFORMATION IS ENCRYPTED AT REST AND IN TRANSIT.
- **Nutrition Data:** Meal logs, protein intake, calorie tracking, hydration logs, fiber intake, food preferences, barcode scan history
- **Symptom Data:** Self-reported symptoms including nausea, food aversions, energy levels, digestive comfort. THIS INFORMATION IS ENCRYPTED AT REST AND IN TRANSIT.
- **Body Composition Data:** Weight, body fat percentage (if provided), lean mass estimates
- **Community Content:** Discussion board posts, comments, shared recipes (if community features are active)

### 2.2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, screens viewed, session duration, interaction patterns
- **Crash Data:** App crash logs and performance diagnostics

### 2.2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Apple HealthKit:** With your explicit permission, we may read weight, body composition, workout data, and step count from HealthKit. We NEVER write data to HealthKit without your explicit permission. HealthKit data is NEVER used for advertising or marketing purposes.
- **Nutrition Databases:** When you scan a barcode or search for a food, we query third-party nutrition databases. These queries do not contain your personal information.

## 2.3 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Generate personalized meal plans and nutrition guidance
- Power the AI assistant (Mira) with contextual awareness of your preferences and goals
- Track your nutritional progress and provide insights
- Generate grocery lists based on your meal plans
- Provide barcode scan verdicts relevant to your dietary profile
- Generate provider reports at your request
- Send notifications (with your permission) for hydration reminders, protein targets, and meal suggestions
- Improve the App and develop new features
- Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

## 2.4 How We Do NOT Use Your Information

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to any third party. Ever.
- Use your health, medication, or nutrition data for advertising or marketing purposes
- Share your medication information with any third party (including pharmacies, pharmaceutical companies, insurance companies, or data brokers)
- Use HealthKit data for any purpose other than providing App functionality you requested
- Store raw audio from voice interactions beyond the duration needed for transcription
- Create de-identified datasets from medication data for sale or licensing

## 2.5 AI Processing

When you interact with Mira (the AI assistant), your prompts are sent to Amazon Web Services (AWS) Bedrock for processing by an AI model (Claude by Anthropic). These prompts include contextual information about your nutritional profile, dietary preferences, and current meal plan state. They do NOT include your full name, email address, or raw medication data. Prompts are not stored by the AI provider beyond the duration of the request.

**Mira conversations are ephemeral by design.** MemoryAisle does not store your Mira conversations on its servers and does not write them to your device's persistent storage. Conversation history is held only in the active app session's memory and is discarded when you close the conversation, background the app for an extended period, or restart the app. There is no "past conversations" archive, no server-side log of message content, and no way to recover a previous Mira chat. Each interaction is meant to live in the moment.

## 2.6 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of all data in transit using TLS 1.3
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest using AES-256 (medication data, symptom data, health information)
- Authentication via Amazon Cognito with JSON Web Tokens (JWT)
- API rate limiting and WAF (Web Application Firewall) protection
- Credentials stored in iOS Keychain, not in application storage
- No personal health data stored in iCloud

## 2.7 Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained for the duration of your account plus 30 days after deletion request
- **Nutrition logs:** Retained for the duration of your account
- **Voice transcriptions:** Processed in real-time and not stored after transcription is complete
- **Mira conversations:** Not retained. Each chat lives only in your device's memory during the active app session and is discarded when the conversation closes, the app is backgrounded for an extended period, or the app restarts. No server-side log of conversation content exists.
- **Barcode scan results:** Cached locally on device for 30 days for offline access
- **Community posts:** Retained until deleted by you or removed by moderation

## 2.8 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of all your data at any time through the App (Profile > Delete Account) or by emailing legal@memoryaisle.app. Upon receiving a deletion request:
- All server-side data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours
- Local data on your device will be cleared immediately upon account deletion in the App
- Community posts you created will be anonymized (username replaced with "Deleted User")
- This action is irreversible

## 2.9 Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at legal@memoryaisle.app and we will promptly delete such information.
